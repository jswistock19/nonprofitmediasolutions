Our Mission

Non-Profits Deserve the Main Stage

Non-Profit Media Solutions is a dedicated 24/7 public space TV network committed to serving the nonprofit community. We offer a guaranteed audience that continues to grow year over year — now you can create a unique connection to viewers everywhere.

Our network features non-profit messages as the main event, not an afterthought. Unlike traditional advertising, your cause gets real screen time where real people gather — from Denny's restaurants to community centers nationwide.

  • Guaranteed high-traffic anchor locations including Denny's restaurants delivering your message 24 hours a day
  • Multiple screens per location for added visibility and maximum impression counts per visit
  • Fully automated systems with branding kits and guidance to increase fundraising and community impact
  • Live event coverage with little to no cost — connecting your cause to your community in real time
