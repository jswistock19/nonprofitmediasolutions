Non-Profit Media Solutions exists to close the gap between worthy causes and the audiences who care about them. We're the only 24/7 dedicated public space TV network built exclusively to serve nonprofits, charities, and cause-driven organizations.

Through our anchor placement in Denny's restaurants nationwide, plus digital streaming and partner networks, your message reaches real people — every hour, every day, in spaces they trust.
